About Us

Welcome to Unistay, your gateway to premium student accommodation in Georgia! As a newly established housing company, we offer international students European-standard living spaces right here in Tbilisi. Our goal is to create a home away from home, ensuring comfort, reliability, and a seamless living experience throughout your academic journey.


We are proud to have partnerships with top universities such as Georgian American University (GAU) and BAU International University. By collaborating with these esteemed institutions, we aim to provide students with convenient, high-quality housing options that support their academic success and personal well-being.


At Unistay, your comfort is our top priority. Our accommodations are fully equipped with modern amenities, ensuring you have everything you need to thrive. We are also committed to expanding our presence, with plans to open new student housing facilities in Batumi and Kutaisi soon.

FAQs

Do you provide female only accommodation?

- Yes, we offer both female-only and male-only accommodation options to ensure comfort and privacy for all students. You can choose the type of accommodation that best suits your preferences when booking.


Can I chose who I live with?

- Yes, in most cases, students can choose who they live with. If you're applying with friends or have specific preferences, we do our best to accommodate your requests based on availability. However, this is subject to room availability at the time of booking.


What's the minimum stay period?

- The minimum stay period is one full academic year. This ensures students have a stable and comfortable living environment throughout their studies.


What common facilities are available (laundry, gym, study rooms, etc.)?

- At our student accommodation, we provide convenient on-site laundry facilities and quiet, dedicated study rooms for your academic needs. While we don't have a gym, there are several fitness centers located nearby, so you'll have easy access to workout options off-campus.


Is there an age limit for booking with Unistay?

- We only accept bookings if you are over 18. This is due to the legally binding tenancy agreement that every tenant is required to sign when living at CRM Students. We also can only accept people in full time education.


Who cleans the room and the communal area?

- We provide cleaning services for both the communal areas and your room once a week. This helps maintain a clean and comfortable living environment, allowing you to focus on your studies. However, we encourage students to keep their spaces tidy in between cleanings.


Can I smoke in the accommodation?

- Smoking is not allowed inside the building. However, you can smoke in designated areas, such as balconies and outdoor spaces. If you smoke inside, the smoke alarms will be activated, leading to potential safety disruptions and penalties.


Can I have pets?

- No, pets are not allowed in our accommodation. This policy helps maintain a clean, comfortable, and allergy-friendly environment for all students.


What happens if I dont get on with my new flatmates? Can I swap rooms?

- If you are having any issues please contact the our team in the first instance, we will do all we can to help. In exceptional circumstances it may be possible to move you; however we would look to resolve any issues with you all.


I have booked a room but failed to obtain a visa, can I cancel my booking?

- Yes you are entitled to cancel your booking but we will need you to provide proof for this.


Can you arrange an airport pick up service?

- Yes, we arrange airport pick-up services for all students. You just need to let us know your name, arrival date and time and your flight number. Please ensure you have enough Georgian Lari in case of an emergency.


How can I make a payment?

- You can pay via Recurring Card, Direct Debit or via Bank Transfer. Cash and Cheques will not be accepted and will be turned away if presented at site. Recurring Card payments can be selected at the time of booking and Direct Debit can be selected by the online portal. If an alternative method of payment is required, such as a bank transfer, or you wish to change your payment method, please contact the Accommodation Team directly for details.
